About Dream Tank

Building systems that transform youth voice into civic action

Our Mission

Dream Tank partners with cities, campuses, and communities to transform lived experience into practical systems, tools, and pathways for care, safety, and civic renewal. We believe that youth-led systems change is not just possible—it's essential for building resilient, responsive communities that can navigate both ordinary times and extraordinary challenges.

Core Values

Youth Agency

Young people are designers, not advisors

Trauma-Informed

Building psychological safety into every process

Systems Thinking

Understanding interconnected impacts and leverage points

Community-Centered

Solutions emerge from lived experience

Action-Oriented

Pilot-ready outputs, not symbolic gestures

Sustainable

Systems built to outlive funding cycles

Our Approach

Listen First

We start by deeply understanding community needs, trust gaps, and existing informal systems through youth-led listening processes.

Map Systems

We identify decision pathways, communication flows, friction points, and leverage points for meaningful change.

Design Solutions

Youth translate insights into protocols, tools, and pilot initiatives grounded in community wisdom.

Build & Test

We prototype interventions with real stakeholders, refining through rapid iteration and feedback.

Learn & Share

Insights are documented and shared across communities, building a collective knowledge base.

Sustain Change

We establish accountability structures and repeatable systems that continue beyond initial implementation.

Impact Areas

Our Labs address challenges across all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals, with particular focus on creating pathways from crisis response to long-term thriving. We work at the intersection of youth development, civic infrastructure, trauma healing, and systems innovation.
