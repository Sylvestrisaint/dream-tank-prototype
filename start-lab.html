Start a Systems Change Lab

Dream Tank partners with cities, universities, and communities to launch local Systems Change Labs that respond to urgent challenges and build long-term civic infrastructure.

Step 1: Choose Your Entry Pathway

How is your community entering the Lab?

OPTION A

Response Lab

Heal Dream Build

Use this pathway when:

  • A mass incident, trauma, or disruption has occurred
  • People do not feel safe, heard, or informed
  • Trust and communication have broken down
  • "Returning to normal" would deepen harm

This pathway prioritizes psychological safety, collective processing, and rapid, visible change.

OPTION B

Prevention Lab

Dream Design Launch

Use this pathway when:

  • You want to build youth agency before crisis
  • You're strengthening trust and participation
  • You're investing in future civic leadership
  • You want systems that can pivot in emergencies

This pathway builds long-term civic capacity.

OPTION C

Systems Upgrade Lab

Use this pathway when:

  • Systems are fragmented or outdated
  • Youth experience is disconnected from decision-making
  • You want to modernize governance, communication, or coordination
